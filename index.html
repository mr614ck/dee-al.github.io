Are you a developer exploring an awesome name for your personal website and projects? You came to the right place!

dee.al is a free identity for developers on the web. This service offers a handy subdomain service for you to have a subdomain like foo.dee.al or bar.dee.al for use on your static sites via CNAME DNS record. Fork, add subdomain and send a Pull Request. We will make it work!
